## Style Guidelines for AI Generation or Artist Commission:

1. **Consistent historical art style** across all images (watercolor, oil painting, or historical illustration)
2. **Respectful and dignified portrayal** of Cherokee people—avoid stereotypes
3. **Period-accurate clothing** (1830s) mixing traditional Cherokee and contemporary American styles
4. **Emotional depth** in facial expressions and body language
5. **Symbolic use of light and color**:
   - Warm tones for hope and community (early scenes)
   - Cold, bleak tones for tragedy (Trail of Tears scenes)
   - Dramatic lighting for pivotal moments
6. **Clear visual storytelling**—each image should convey the scene's meaning at a glance
7. **Avoid gratuitous violence**—show consequences and suffering with dignity, not gore
